tawar

1. 1) tasteless, insipid. 2) ineffective (of poison, magic spells, incantations, etc.). 3) undisturbed, at an even temper. 2. bargain.

sinonim: payau, adem, anyep, campah, cemplang, hambar (rasa), basi, batal, batil, reda, surut, dingin , kering , hampa, kecut, sumbing, netral, cak menawar,
