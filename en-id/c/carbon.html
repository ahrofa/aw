carbon

The chemical element of atomic number 6, a non-metal which has two main forms (diamond and graphite) and which also occurs in impure form in charcoal, soot, and coal.

hydrocarbonn.

A compound containing only hydrogen and carbon, as methane, benzene, etc.; also, by extension, any of their derivatives.

hydrocarbonaceousa.

Of the nature, or containing, hydrocarbons.

hydrocarbonaten.

A hydrous carbonate, as malachite.

hydrocarbonaten.

A hydrocarbon.

recarbonizev. t.

To restore carbon to; as, to recarbonize iron in converting it into steel.
